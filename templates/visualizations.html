Energy Visualizations

Energy Consumption Over Time

This graph shows the trend of energy consumption over a specified period. By analyzing this data, we can identify patterns such as peak usage times and seasonal variations in energy demand.

Contribution of Each Energy Source

This chart illustrates the proportion of energy generated from different sources such as solar, wind, and hydro. Understanding the contribution of each source helps in optimizing energy mix and improving sustainability.

Correlation Heatmap

The correlation heatmap displays the relationships between various factors affecting energy consumption, such as temperature, humidity, and wind speed. Darker colors indicate stronger correlations, which can be used to predict energy demand more accurately.

Load vs Temperature

This graph demonstrates the relationship between energy load and temperature. Typically, energy consumption increases with temperature due to the use of cooling systems. This visualization helps in understanding how temperature variations impact energy demand.
